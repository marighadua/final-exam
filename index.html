What is KiKi?!

KiKi is an online marketplace through which users are able to sell or purchase certain items with other users.

One community, thousands of brands, and a whole lot of second-hand style

We have made it easy to buy clothes and items in a more sustainable way online. By buying and selling second hand, we can save millions of litres of water, avoid tonnes of emissions and put things to better use.

Our goal is to make shopping second hand as easy as possible and accessible for everyone.

This is no small challenge. But we are ready to take it on.
